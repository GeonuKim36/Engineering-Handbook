# Engineering Handbook

> A long-term knowledge base documenting engineering principles, practical implementations, and research insights accumulated throughout my academic and professional journey.

---

## Overview

Engineering Handbook is a continuously evolving collection of engineering knowledge gathered through coursework, research, personal projects, and practical experience.

The purpose of this repository is not simply to store notes, but to organize engineering concepts into a structured, searchable, and reusable reference.

Topics range from fundamental theories to advanced engineering techniques, with an emphasis on understanding both **why** and **how** engineering systems work.

## Goals

The long-term goals of this repository are to:

- Build a comprehensive engineering knowledge base.
- Organize concepts from fundamentals to advanced topics.
- Document practical implementations alongside theoretical backgrounds.
- Preserve knowledge gained through research and development.
- Create reusable references for future projects.
- Share useful engineering resources with the engineering community.

---

## Philosophy

This repository values:

- Understanding over memorization
- First principles thinking
- Practical implementation
- Clear explanations
- Continuous improvement

Every document is expected to evolve as new knowledge and experience are accumulated.
